How do I report a lost item?
Sign in and click on "Report Item". Fill in all the details about the item and submit the form. Our team will assist you.
How can I claim a found item?
Check our list of found items on the website. If you see yours, click "Claim" and provide proof of ownership to verify your claim.
Is there any fee for using the Lost and Found service?
No, the CvSU Naic Lost and Found service is free for all students and staff.
How long are unclaimed items kept?
Items are securely stored for up to 90 days. After that, they may be donated or properly disposed of.
